Greece has been called the framework of western civilization, given that the political practice of democracy
that we use today in our society started with the Greeks over 2000 years ago. Greece is a southeastern Europe
country that has hundreds of its own islands spread across its Aegean sea. It is one of the more popular
civilizations portrayed in our pop culture with its magnificent architecture and mythology. Greece has not only
influenced our political world but also our language, for example, some sayings that we repeat today came
from Greece, sayings like "taking the bull by the horns" was from the Greek myth of Hercules saving Crete
from a Bull by seizing its horns. Greece is famous for being one of the leading producers of olives and have
been cropping olive trees since ancient times.
